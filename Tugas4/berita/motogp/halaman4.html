Devisiozo: the attendance king

The italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years-the third highest on the grid behind Valintino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been-on paper-Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
